Job Responsibilities   As a Medical Assistant you will be responsible for assisting a physician, PA or NP with clerical duties, patient flow, patient care and procedures.   Additional responsibilities may include Front desk reception, patient medical records, general medical office work.   Recording patients' medical history, vital statistics, or information such as test results in medical records. Preparing treatment rooms for patient examinations, keeping the rooms neat and clean. Interviewing patients to obtain medical information and measure their vital signs, weight, and height. Authorizing drug refills and providing prescription information to pharmacies. Cleaning and sterilizing instruments and disposing of contaminated supplies. Showing patients to examination rooms and preparing them for the physician. Helping physicians examine and treat patients, handing them instruments or materials for performing such tasks as giving injections.   Requirements:   Bilingual Medical assistant diploma/certificate and at least 1 year of related experience. Medical Assistant Certification, preferred. Basic Life Support or ability to obtain within 30 days of hire. Basic computer skills, knowledge of Cerner EMR system a plus. Ability to multi - task a must.
