The Toddler Teacher will be responsible for providing a quality educational and childcare experience for toddlers, ensuring their safety, development, and enjoyment.  Other responsibilities will include but are not limited to: • Contributing to monthly theme selection and weekly sub-theme selection • Lesson plan development, submission, and execution for children 2-3 years old • Corresponding with enrolled families and implementation of assessments and conferences  • Maintaining a classroom that is vibrant, engaging, tidy, and affords appropriate opportunities for learning • Maintaining and stocking appropriate educational materials and supplies for classroom use • Seeking out forward thinking educational opportunities and academic approaches for students • Upholding and enforcing all aspects of the DCFS childcare standards and company policies.  Qualifications: *Early Childhood Education degree (or in progress), preferred *At least 1-2 years early childhood experience
