Tech is responsible for performing various assignments within the operating room and sterile processing department to include decontamination, instruments tray preparation, equipment maintenance and supply. Responsibilities also include scrubbing for urology cases, preparing instruments and equipment for cases, maintaining a sterile and safe environment for the patient, preparing cultures for lab, and assist registered nurse in circulating for all surgical procedures as well as other duties as needed.  *Qualifications:*  Graduate of an accredited program or military training program for operating room and central services technicians Current Basic Cardiac Life Support (BCLS) Certified Surgical Technologist (CST) status a plus
