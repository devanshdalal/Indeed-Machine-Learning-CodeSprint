Warehouse/Stock Worker Job Description  Responsible for the accurate put away, picking and loading of raw materials, intermediates and finished product into warehouse locations or onto trailers by performing the following duties.  • Load and unload materials on to and off of a truck.   • Assist in securing the delivery load.   • Perform inventory of all products before and after delivery.   • Assist in delivering materials on time.  Perform other duties as assigned. KEY RESPONSIBILITIES • Prepares orders by processing requests and supply orders; pulling materials; packing boxes; placing orders in delivery area. • Maintains inventory controls by collecting stock location orders and printing requests. • Maintains quality service by following organization standards. • Maintains safe and clean work environment by keeping shelves, pallet area, and workstations neat; maintaining clean shipping supply area; complying with procedures, rules, and regulations. • Contributes to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed. • Knows, and observes all Standard Operating Procedures, safety procedures and rules, and environmental practices. • Assists in all movements in and out of the warehouse. • Assists in maintaining a clean and organized warehouse inside and out. • Works closely with truck drivers to safely load and unload trucks and containers as instructed. • Assists in packaging/re-packaging products for shipping and returned material as instructed. • Makes suggestions for improvements in safety, environmental, and quality of products if you see them. • May perform other related assignments and operate other equipment as determined by supervision. • Move freight, stock, or other materials to and from storage or production areas, loading docks, delivery vehicles, ships, or containers, by hand or using equipment. • Read work orders or receive oral instructions to determine work assignments or material or equipment needs. • Record numbers of units handled or moved, using daily production sheets or work tickets. • Attach identifying tags to containers or mark them with identifying information. • Sort cargo before loading and unloading. • Load material to machine. • Palletize orders (shrink wrap). • Assemble packaging. • Monitor manufacturing.  REQUIREMENTS • Must be on time. • Teamwork, Coordination, Organization, Planning, Time Management, Reporting Skills, Inventory Control, Documentation Skills, Equipment Maintenance, Data Entry Skills, Dependability • Education equivalent to high School diploma or GED; or equivalent combination of education and experience • Minimum one year forklift driving experience required. • Must have a valid driver’s license and clean driving record. • Must be able to become forklift certified. • Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedures and procedure manuals. • Ability to add, subtract, multiply and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions, and decimals. This includes the ability to rate and ratio. • Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form. • Basic computer skills • Self-motivated individual with good time management skills • Excellent organizational skills and ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment. • Must be available to work overtime and weekends on short notice. • Must be 18 years of age • Must have viable transportation • Must be able to lift and carry up to 60 lbs. repetitively.  • Must be able to turn off person phone during work hours (emergency – may call office) • Must be alert. • Able to pass background checks and drug screens.
