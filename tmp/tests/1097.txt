POSITION SUMMARY   Develop programs to control machining of parts by CNC lathes and mills    ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS   •Tool identification – Document each tool used for individual machines and processes.  •Tool availability/distribution – Closely work with tool vendors to insure proper quantities are available to the shop either in the crib or tool boss system.  •Tool testing/selection – Evaluate price and production runtime calculations per tool to produce best value for the company.    •Program sequence - Determine the order of machine operations, and select the proper cutting tools needed to machine work pieces into the desired shapes, surface finishes and tolerances.   •Program revisions - Revise programs and to eliminate errors and match drawing revisions. Retest programs to verify that potential problems have been solved.  •Tool/program analysis - Analyze job orders, drawings, specifications and design data in order to determine tool selection, machine speeds and feed rates.   •Program geometry - Determine reference points, machine cutting paths, hole locations and compute angular and linear dimensions, radii and curvatures.   •Prove programs - Observe machines on trial runs to ensure that programs and machinery will function properly and produce items that meet specifications.   •Continuous improvement - Modify existing programs to enhance machine cutting speed and efficiency.     POSITION QUALIFICATIONS   Competency Statement(s)   •Accuracy - Ability to perform work accurately and thoroughly.  •Analytical Skills - Ability to use thinking and reasoning to solve a problem effectively.  •Technical Aptitude - Ability to comprehend complex technical topics and specialized information.  •Safety Awareness - Ability to identify and correct conditions that affect employee safety.  •Accountability - Ability to accept responsibility and account for his/her actions.  •Persistence - Ability to complete tasks or continue in a course of action in spite of opposition or discouragement.    SKILLS & ABILITIES   Education: Associate's Degree (two year college or technical school)   Experience: Two to four years related experience   Computer Skills   Pro E, Pro Manufacture
