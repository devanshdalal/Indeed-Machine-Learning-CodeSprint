As a Child Center Associate, you will provide members and their children with a safe, healthy and inviting environment in which to grow and learn.    Responsibilities -Check children in and out of Child Center utilizing our club software. Responsible for the required paperwork for new members.  Occasional front desk  - Implement and organize fun and entertaining activities and games for children. - Monitor and interact with children to maintain a safe and healthy environment with respect to the toys, other children, and surroundings - Clean and maintain the Child Center by following the cleaning checklist and performing assigned tasks.  Qualifications - Working toward or obtained a high school diploma or GED - One year of experience working with children preferred - Infant/Child and Adult CPR/AED certification required within first 60 days of employment
