The Customer Service Representative serves as the primary business contact for the client and is responsible for client satisfaction. The CSR is expected to consistently provide excellent customer service to accounts, as well as represent client needs and goals within the organization to ensure quality. In addition, the CSR should build relationships with clients to encourage new and repeat business opportunities.  Primary Duties: •  Interacts with customers by phone, email, or in person and receives orders or changes in service. •  Processes and inputs all customer orders and receipts.  Runs stock status reports to check for product availability.  •  Send requested reports to the customer each day.   •  Assures quality standards and client expectations are met. •  Assures that client issues are dealt with in an efficient manner. •  Assures that weekly billing templates are up to date  •  Works in coordination with the warehouse team and shipping team. •  Informs management of customers change requests or expedited orders.  •  Provides regular two-way communication between the client and team, to provide strong team representation and set proper client expectations. •  Understanding of company capabilities and service, and effectively communicates all offerings to the client.  Experience: •  Minimum of 3 years’ experience as a Customer Service Representative, of which at least one year must have been in a warehouse or Distribution Center.  •  Supply chain management experience (domestic and international) •  Minimum 3 years of experience with warehouse computer management systems. •  Experience with routing guides and compliance •  Experience working with both LTL and FTL carriers and their systems.    Essential Skills: •  PC literate with experience with Microsoft Outlook, Word and Excel  •  Ability to write reports and business correspondence. •  Ability to present information to customers both internal and external.  •  Very strong excel skills.  •  Ability to solve complex problems with a focus on attention to detail.
