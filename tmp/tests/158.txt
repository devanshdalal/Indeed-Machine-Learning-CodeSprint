*Title:* IT Service Desk Analyst *Type:* Long term (possibility of contract to hire)  *Location:* Purchase, NY *Pay Rate:* $18 to $24 per hour (possibility of contract to hire - $45 to $55K base salary depending on experience and skill set)   *Summary:*  The IT Service Desk Analyst will provide first level technical support to internal employees and contractors. The successful candidate will require an aptitude for working with applications/systems to undertake analysis, diagnosis and resolution of employees and contractors problems, which may range from straightforward to more complicated technical issues. There is also a range of administration duties within this role. Coverage includes the main HQ site at Purchase, NY as well as remote users at other offices and home workers.  *Responsibilities:*  • Act as a single point of contact for phone calls and emails from employees and contractors regarding IT issues and queries • Receiving, logging and managing calls from internal employees and contractors via telephone, email, voicemail and IM • Maintaining an Asset Database and track changes • 1st level support - troubleshooting of IT related problems from in-house software to hardware, such as Blackberrys, Laptops, PCs, Printers, iPads, iPhones and LAN • Troubleshoot basic network issues such as ADSL broadband issues • Escalate unresolved calls to the next level of support • Log all calls in the Service Desk Call Logging system (BMC SDE) • Take ownership of user problems and follow up the status of problems on behalf of the user and communicate progress in a timely manner to maintain a high degree of customer service for all support queries and adhere to all service management principles • Provide basic in-house training in MS Office applications used within the Association (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint) • Provide stats for the weekly Service Desk report on call trends • Publishing support documentation to assist employees and contractors with requests for information & provide training if required • Basic Active Directory knowledge. Creating user accounts, reset passwords, create groups etc. • Blackberry account management and provision (BES)  *Qualifications:*  • Two plus years of Helpdesk experience. • An ITIL qualification is preferable but not essential • Working knowledge of ITIL Incident, Problem and Change Management • MCP certification would be desirable • MCP/MCSE and advantage but not essential. • Network hardware knowledge. • A+ qualification an advantage  *Requirements:* • Excellent communication skills and telephone manner. • Excellent organizational skills • Incident Management experience – Managing incidents including business expectations and communication • Basic User & Security Group Active Directory administration • Strong knowledge of Microsoft based operating systems with emphasis on Windows 7 and Office 2007 • Experience with using and troubleshooting Outlook 2007 within a network environment (permissions, calendar sharing, delegation) • You will be a self-motivated achiever who gains satisfaction from providing excellent customer service • Knowledge of Spanish would be useful.  *Additional Info:* Hours of Service Desk operation - 6:30am to 10pm Mon to Fri, and 10am to 7pm Sat to Sun Shift will be during these hours Some call out for IT support required.
