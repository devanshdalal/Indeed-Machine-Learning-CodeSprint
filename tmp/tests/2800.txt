TotalMed Staffing is seeking candidates for a full time Administrative Office Clerk in Wausau, Wisconsin.  The responsibilities of an Administrative Office Clerk would be to triage incoming calls and incoming mail as well as general clerical duties. Other responsibilities of the Administrative Office Clerk would be to log claims appeals into an online application. This is an entry level contract position, and pay rate will vary based on experience.    The requirement for the Administrative Office Clerk position are as follows:  Proficiency with computers 1 or more years of clerical/administrative experience Must be able to speak, read, and write English High School Diploma or equivalent Ability to operate a copier, calculator, and personal computer applications
