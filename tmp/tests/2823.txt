_JOB OBJECTIVES_ Contributes to the success of the organization by maintaining all aspects of accounting responsibility for construction and development departments.  _ESSENTIAL DUTIES_ * Complete job cost accounting duties for assigned projects * Review and maintain AIA documentation. * Prepare construction monthly draws. * Complete monthly general ledger account analysis and loan reconciliation. * Complete other detailed accounting analyses as requested. * Complete monthly bank reconciliations in an accurate and timely manner. * Research and resolve discrepancies, as needed. * Complete monthly filing and dissemination of accounting documents. * Assist with Junior Accountant duties as needed * Conform with and abide by all regulations, policies, practices, work procedures and instructions. * Perform additional duties as assigned.  _ESSENTIAL KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES_ * Knowledge of generally accepted accounting principles. * Effective organizational and time management skills. * Excellent verbal and written communication and listening skills. * Ability to work independently with minimal supervision. * Accurate and detail oriented. * Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to interact and relate effectively, productively, and     positively with other Associates. * Dependable, punctual, reliable. * Ability to work a schedule that may include hours other than 8-5 Monday through Friday, depending on business needs.  _ESSENTIAL TECHNICAL SKILLS_ * Knowledge of or ability to learn Construction accounting processes, including use of any industry specific software. * Typing and data entry skills. * Ability to use a 10-key calculator accurately by touch. * Proficient in Word, Excel, Outlook, and accounting systems, including job cost systems.  _DESIRED EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE_ * B.A./B.S. degree in Accounting/Finance or related field. * One year of previous accounting experience. * Timberline Software experience      Related
