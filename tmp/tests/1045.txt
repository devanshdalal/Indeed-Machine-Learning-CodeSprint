Description: As the Accounting Manager based in West Chester, OH, you will perform tasks requiring you to be responsible for developing and maintaining accounting principles, practices and procedures to ensure accurate and timely financial statements.  The Accounting Manager will supervise accounting personnel and is responsible for managing the team to ensure work is properly allocated and completed in a timely manner.  In addition, the Accounting Manager will work closely with the company's external CPA firm.  This position addresses tight deadlines and a multitude of accounting activities including general ledger preparation, financial reporting, year end audit preparation, tax preparation and the support of budget and forecast activities.  The Accounting Manager will have contact with the company's Legal Counsel, other department managers and reports directly to the CEO, this position therefore requires strong interpersonal communication skills both written and verbal.  QuoteGuardian.com, LLC is one of the most successful online lead generation providers to the US Insurance industry. We use internet marketing to turn consumer interest in insurance products into business growth for many of the largest insurance carriers and their agents in the world. We are a tight-knit team with a fast-paced, energetic, and entrepreneurial company culture. We provide competitive pay, excellent benefits, and a fun and collaborative work environment.  Responsibilities and Duties:      Obtain and maintain a thorough understanding of the financial reporting and general ledger structure.     Ensure an accurate and timely monthly, quarterly and year end close.     Ensure the timely reporting of all monthly financial information.     Ensure all tax payments and documents are submitted on time and accurately.     Ensure all invoices are paid and recorded on time.     Ensure all Receivables are paid and recorded on time.     Monitors and analyzes department work to develop more efficient procedures and use of resources while maintaining a high level of accuracy.     Advises staff regarding the handling of non-routine reporting transactions.     Assist in development and implementation of new procedures and features to enhance the workflow of the department.     Work closely with other department managers to develop department budgets.     Monitor work and development of accounting and finance staff.  Minimum Requirements:      BA/BS in Business with an emphasis in Accounting, CPA required     Two to Three years prior supervisory experience in the financial reporting/general ledger area.  Experience working in a start up is preferred.     Must be PC proficient and able to thrive in a fast-pace setting.  Experience with automated accounting systems is required.  Must have strong experience with Microsoft Excel, Access and Word.
