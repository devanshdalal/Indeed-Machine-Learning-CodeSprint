Company Information  The Rabine Group is a local, regional, and national provider of commercial paving, commercial roofing, industrial flooring, fuel, and oil distribution services. The Rabine Group has been recognized by Crain’s Chicago Business as one of the largest privately held companies in Chicago and as one of the 50 fastest growing companies in Chicago.    With a firm commitment to quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction, the Rabine Group companies include: Rabine Paving, Rabine Paving America, Pavement Solutions, Rabine Utility Paving, R. Commercial Roofing Solutions, Crawford Roofing Experts, Rabine Sealco, Rabine Snow Pros, Pipe View, Palatine Oil Company and Primekss Rabine.   Job Opportunity  The Rabine Group is seeking a Controller to work in the Schaumburg location. The Controller is a key member of the Management team, reporting into the Chief Operations Officer.  This individual will assume a strategic role in the overall financial management of the company.  The Controller will have primary day to day responsibility for planning, implementing, managing and controlling all financial-related activities of the company.  This will include direct and indirect responsibility for accounting, finance, forecasting, property management, deal analysis and negotiations, investor relationships and institutional financing.   Essential Functions  • Provides leadership in the development for the continuous evaluation of short and long term strategic financial objectives. • Ensure credibility of Finance group by providing timely and accurate analysis of budgets, financial trends and forecasts. • Direct and oversee all aspects of the Finance and Accounting functions of the organization. • Evaluates and advises on the impact of long range planning, introduction of new programs/strategies and regulatory action and merger and acquisition activity. • Establish and maintain strong relationships with senior executives so as to identify their needs and collaborate to seek a full range of business solutions. • Manage and direct processes for financial forecasting, budgets and financial reporting to the company. • Provide recommendations to strategically enhance financial performance and business opportunities. • Ensure that effective internal controls are in place and ensure compliance with GAAP and applicable federal, state, and local regulatory laws and rules for financial and tax reporting. • Direct and monitor all banking activities and relationships. • Motivate, manage and develop a strong team of finance and accounting professionals. • Manage and coach the Accounting team by establishing comprehensive goals for the team according to the needs of the business.  • Mirror The Rabine Groups values, mission, vision, to all employees. • Use external presence and relationships to garner new opportunities. • Establish an environment of learning and growth within the organization.  Requirements  • Bachelor’s degree in relevant area required (Finance, Accounting, Economics and/or Business). • 5-7 years experienced leader within the accounting field required. • Excellence in organizational management with the ability to coach staff, manage, and develop high-performance teams, set and achieve strategic objectives, and manage a budget required. • An intelligent and articulate individual who can relate to people at all levels of an organization and possesses excellent communication skills required. • Must be able to read, write and speak English. • Ability to remain calm in stressful situations. • Commitment to The Rabine Group’s core values, mission, and programs is required.
