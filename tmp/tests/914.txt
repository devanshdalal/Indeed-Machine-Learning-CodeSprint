Mortgage Customer Service:   Boston Community Capital is seeking to hire an individual to work in our Dudley Square, Roxbury office to lead customer service for the SUN (Stabilizing Urban Neighborhoods) Initiative -- an innovative approach to foreclosure relief that reduces monthly mortgage expense and helps foreclosed borrowers in Massachusetts maintain home ownership of their homes.  Candidates must have a four year college degree with at least a 3.0/4.0 GPA. Working knowledge of Excel is also required. While we are willing to train the right candidate, candidates with some experience in customer service is a plus. Applicants should have at least 2 years of professional work experience. Duties will include working with clients to help keep them current on their mortgage, resolving client payment problems and assisting with transitioning clients for those unable to keep up with their mortgage. You will also assist with handing off loans to our third party Servicing Organization.  Candidates must possess strong written and oral communication skills and must be able to work in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment.  Candidates must be capable of multi-tasking. Our target markets are families and individuals who are behind on their mortgage payments, facing eviction and/or in foreclosure and who live in MA. Compensation is commensurate with experience with range between $38K-$48K.   Boston Community Capital is an equal opportunity employer.
