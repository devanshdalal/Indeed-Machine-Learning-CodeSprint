*Summary of Responsibilities:*  Under direction of the Controller, the Staff Accountant - Collections reviews and analyzes client accounts for accuracy and makes collection calls. Prepares and keys billings/credits for clients in specified territory of responsibility.  Receives and applies payments to client accounts. Makes credit decisions on based on customer history. Files and sends out all account related documentation.     *Essential Functions:*  •Makes collection calls to clients based on established priority list.   •Researches any invoicing discrepancies and makes any necessary corrections.   •Prepares miscellaneous credits/billings per established guidelines and keys them to client's account in an accurate and timely manner.   •Calls client with any question regarding discrepancies or short payments.   •Under direction of the Controller, makes credit decisions regarding based on client history and current status.   •Completes credit inquiries on client accounts in territory of responsibility.   •Contacts clients with past due accounts and collects payments.  •Reviews open accounts for collection efforts and makes decisions on pending services, closing accounts and extending additional credit.   •Communicates with internal and external customers by telephone, email and postal mail to investigate complaints, verify accuracy of charges and/or to correct errors in accounts.   •Makes changes to client files in time and billing system as needed, such as staff assignments, address/phone/ contact changes and project or engagement additions, deletions or consolidations.  •Answers Partners and other assigned billers invoicing questions.    •Prepares or assists with client billings under the guidance of the partners.   •Works with third parties in placing client accounts for collection, negotiating settlements and/or establishing legal action.  Acts as the company representative in all collection legal proceedings  •Prepares analyses and reports for Management and others regarding WIP and AR, including agings, collection effectiveness, write-offs, AR Hold list etc.  •Meet with Controller to discuss any problem with AR & WIP accounts and suggest resolution methods.  •Performs other related duties as required.     *Education & Experience Required:*  •High School Diploma required.  Bachelors degree strongly preferred.   •Three to five years credit/accounting experience or equivalent combination of education and experience obtained in a *Professional Services* setting.  •Ability to use various computer software in the Windows environment.  •*Basic knowledge of accounting practices and procedures.*  •Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as interest and percentages.  •Excellent analytical and communication skills  •Excellent interpersonal skills.  •Strong written communication skills.  •Effectively communicate with internal and external customers.  •Ability to interact within a team environment.   •Ability to maintain confidentiality.    *Qualifications / Requirements:*  To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies to perform the essential functions of this position:  •Analytical—the individual synthesizes complex or diverse information.   •Problem solving—the individual identifies and resolves problems in a timely manner and gathers and analyzes information skillfully.  •Communication—the individual speaks and writes clearly and persuasively in positive or negative situations, demonstrates group presentation skills and conducts productive meetings.   •Delegation—the individual delegates work assignments, gives authority to work independently, and sets expectations and monitors delegated activities.   •Quality management—the individual looks for ways to improve and promote quality and demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness.   •Judgment—the individual displays willingness to make decisions, exhibits sound and accurate judgment and makes timely decisions.   •Planning/organizing—the individual prioritizes and plans work activities, uses time efficiently and develops realistic action plans.  •Consistently reports to work on time, follows instructions, responds to management direction and solicits feedback to improve performance.
