Primary duties include composite repair of aerospace fiberglass products. Must be familiar with basic tools related to this position. Sanding, potting of inserts, filling, laminate repair and vacuum bagging as required in the repair process. Must be familiar with standard concepts, practices and procedures in the aircraft composite field. Must be able to read and interpret blue print dimensions and specifications. Will work under general supervision reporting to a supervisor. A minimum of 1 year experience is required.
