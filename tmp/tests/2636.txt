Description  St. Paul’s Episcopal Montessori School is a ministry and outreach of St. Paul’s Episcopal Church, a historic downtown parish founded in 1883. The school was founded in 2005 and has grown from 5 students to 105 (ages 18 months to 12 years.) This is a very vibrant and culturally and economically diverse community. A full time assistant elementary teacher is required for the fall. Some teaching experience required, Montessori preferred. Great opportunity for student teacher interested in Montessori. Benefits include health insurance, pension, paid sick days and training Applicants should email resume and cover letter
