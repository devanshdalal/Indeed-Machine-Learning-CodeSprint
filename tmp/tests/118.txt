PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITY:  Centerless Grinder Machinist individual Sets up and operate precision grinding equipment to grind lot parts in accordance with established shop practices. Performs work operations under supervision.    ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:   • Sets-up and operates precision grinding equipment to perform operations within machine capacity and capability.   • Selects tooling and fixtures pertaining to the grinding operation.   • Inspects parts for quality and appropriate tolerance using measuring tools such as micrometers, calipers, and comparators.   • Determines proper feed and speed combination. May true and shape grinding and regulating wheels as required.   • Records the piece count and run time of the production times.   • Recognizes improper machine functioning and makes required adjustments if possible.   • Demonstrated technical knowledge and experience in reading and interpreting blueprints and use shop and measuring tools.   • Demonstrated ability to use SPC and shop floor data collection systems.   • Other duties may be assigned.
