*Job Description*  The Business Solutions Sales Executive for Ben Jen Online works to sell goods for suppliers. A technical background is required for this position as is advanced knowledge of electronics, since the items sold by BJO are of that nature. The Business Solutions Sales Executive will contact both new and existing customers in order to discuss their needs and to explain how these needs could be met by specific products and services. The ideal executive will be able to answer customers' questions about products, prices, availability, product uses, and credit terms as directed. Executives will be trained to use BJO systems for quoting prices, credit terms, and other bid specifications. He or she will also use sales techniques to emphasize product features based on an analysis of customers' needs and upon his or her technical knowledge of the product capabilities and limitations. The ideal executive is able to negotiate prices, terms of sale, and service agreements. The Business Solutions Sales Executive must also maintain customer records using automated systems as well as identify prospective customers by using business directories, following leads from existing clients, participating in organizations and clubs, and attending trade shows and conferences. This individual will prepare sales contracts for orders obtained and submit orders for processing.
