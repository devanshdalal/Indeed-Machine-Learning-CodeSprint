Headquartered in Ramsey, Minnesota, Vision-Ease Lens is a manufacturer of quality ophthalmic lenses.  Vision-Ease Lens products are sold through independent opticians, optometrists and ophthalmologists, as well as many retail chains and dispensing markets across the country.    The SR. MARKET ANALYST is responsible for performing independent and comprehensive analytical studies in support of the Sales and Marketing group. This person understands the needs of the business in order to provide accurate and actionable information as it relates to sales compensation, sales forecasts, market segmentation and marketing initiatives. By gathering and analyzing the market, this individual will help management use the information in justifying and assessing strategic initiatives as well as market opportunities.    Requirements:   • Bachelor's degree (B. A.) from four-year college or university; or two to four years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Preferred degree in marketing, business, finance, accounting, mathematics, statistics or economics.   • Solid experience managing and analyzing large datasets using Excel or Access.   • Experience using SQL-based software programs (Microstrategy, Business Objects, etc.).     Preferred:   • 2-3 years of related experience.   • Demand planning experience in the manufacturing industry.   • Past experience creating presentations for management.   • Experience supporting and collaborating with organization’s sales force.   • Experience creating or working with sales commission payments.   • Experience with Excel VBA and Pivot Tables.   • Customer relationship management experience (Salesforce.com, Microsoft CRM, etc.).
