Overview Vertical Nerve (www.example.com) is a digital optimization firm with core areas of expertise in Digital Analytics, Search Marketing, Conversion Optimization and Web Development. Vertical Nerve is seeking an experienced Project Manager to manage web-related projects (website builds, changes, designs, etc..).  Role The Project Manager acts an integral member of the team and is responsible for planning, executing, and finalizing projects according to strict deadlines and within budget. This includes acquiring and managing resources, and coordinating the efforts of team members and third-party contractors in order to deliver projects according to plan. The Project Manager will also define the project’s objectives and oversee quality control throughout its life cycle.  The individual will be required to have excellent communication skills, the ability to quickly take on responsibility and enjoy working in a challenging and fast-paced environment.  The right candidate will be a fast worker who is also technically savvy, organized and responsive.  Organization  The Project Manager works closely with the creative, development, QA and implementation resources.
