Purpose of the job: The Retail Sales Consultant (RSC) will be responsible for assisting new and existing customers with the purchase of telecommunications/technology products and services. The RSC is recognized by Prime Communications as an exemplary sales associate, consistently delivering high quality operations and excellent customer satisfaction levels. This individual must consistently achieve sales, revenue and income objectives, while meeting or exceeding monthly quotas. The RSC will work with appropriate departments or business partners to ensure that high levels of customer satisfaction are met in every area. The RSC is responsible for store appearance and various aspects of office administration, as well as assisting in special projects assigned by the supervisor or other duties as assigned by management. Other responsibilities include cash handling, daily deposits and inventory procedures.
