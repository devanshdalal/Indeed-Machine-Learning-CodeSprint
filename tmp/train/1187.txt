  Job Description    **Truck Driver will drive along and around SE Iowa - Daily Deliveries **  JOB TITLE: Truck Driver  OBJECTIVE: Operates vehicles to transport parts, equipment and supplies for the dealership.  May instruct customers on basic, equipment operating procedures.   ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:  o Transports equipment to/from customers and/or between dealer facilities; may also pick-up new equipment from a John Deere factory o Supports the sales department with equipment delivery as needed o Maintains records of pickup and deliveries and obtains necessary signatures and receipts o Maintains all required certifications in line with DOT, OSHA and company policy  o Accounts for all time and for all distance traveled in performing assigned duties o May need to maintain assigned trucks and related equipment in good condition o May instruct customers on basic equipment operating procedures as required  The above statements cover what are generally believed to be the principal and essential functions of this job.  Specific circumstances may allow or require some people assigned to the job to perform a somewhat different combination of duties.    MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS:  Education and Experience: • High School Diploma, GED or equivalent work experience. Technical Knowledge and Skills: • CDL License  • Minimum 1 year experience as semi-trailer truck operator, hauling heavy equipment • Ability to lift at least 75 lbs. repeatedly  Working Conditions: All weather conditions  Travel Requirements: • Daily
