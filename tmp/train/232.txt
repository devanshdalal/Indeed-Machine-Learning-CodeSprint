State-wide mental health agency looking for a Human Resources Assistant with 1-3 years of previous office administration experience – a self-starter with demonstrable communication and organization skills, proficiency with Microsoft Office applications and the ability to complete assignments with speed and minimal supervision.  Incumbent should enjoy thriving in an environment that offers a lot of variety and a fast-pace.  Requires high school diploma.  Bachelor’s degree in HR and/or PHR certification preferred.
