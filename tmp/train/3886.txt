The Showroom Receptionist is a front line representative for the dealership and must represent the dealership in a professional, friendly manner at all times.     Job Responsibilities  * Greet showroom customers and determine the nature of their visit  * Notify the appropriate salestaff when they have a customer waiting  * Answer basic customer inquiries and refer more extensive inquires to appropriate staff  * Answer all incoming calls, screens the calls and determines the appropriate department to transfer calls.   * Communicates with callers in a professional, friendly manner.   * Tracks and logs all dealership vehicle keys     Qualifications  * Must have receptionist experience  * Must have experience with multi-phone lines   * Professional personal apperance a must  * Must speak clearly and pleasantly on the telephone
