The Digital Media Production Associate is responsible for the daily operations of the duplication, printing and finishing processes.  Responsibilities include completion of all jobs on time utilizing the established quality process while insuring high maintenance standards for the equipment.    Essential Duties:    • Setting up and running production for CD’s and DVD duplication.  • Verifying content.  • Perform inventory and quality control audits.  • Assist other areas to meet all deadlines.  • Work safely and adhere to OneTouchPoint CCI policies.    Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Required:    •  Knowledge of color digital printing and finishing.  • Knowledge in Adobe Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat)  • Ability to work in a team-oriented environment.  • Excellent attention to detail and accuracy.  • Ability to multi-task.
