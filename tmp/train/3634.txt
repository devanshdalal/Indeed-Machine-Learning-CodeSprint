*Position Overview* The duties of a Distribution Pipeline Welder will include but are not limited to welding service pipe, ensuring Benton safety standards and assisting in the construction and installation of distribution gas pipeline.  *Essential Job Functions* * Position, align and weld pipes in compliance with Benton and customer policies and standards.  * Bolt, clamp, and tack-weld components to secure the pipe in position for welding.  * Performs basic rigging procedures and steel fabrication. * Ensure and follow Benton Safety Standards and Policies.   *Requirements*  * MUST have experience with LDC in southeastern market of natural gas. * This position will be performed in all types of weather conditions and environments.
